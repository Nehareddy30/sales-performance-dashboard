Key Business Insights
📋 Analyst Findings & Recommendations
Executive Summary
💡 Top Revenue Driver
Technology is the highest-revenue category at $907K (53.7%) of total sales, led by Copiers ($261K) and Laptops ($259K). Prioritize inventory and promotions in this segment.
📍 Regional Opportunity
The South region leads profit at $67.9K, while East lags at $62K despite similar sales volumes. Investigating pricing or discount strategies in the East could improve margins.
⚠️ Furniture Margin Alert
Furniture generates $611K in sales but only 10.2% margin — the lowest of all categories. High discounting on Tables and Chairs is likely eroding profitability. Recommend reviewing discount policies.
📅 Seasonality Pattern
September is the peak month in 2024 ($57.6K), followed by November. Q4 shows strong performance. Q1 (January) is the weakest — consider targeted promotions in Jan–Feb to boost performance.
